Some of our transport means

Looking for a rental car for self drive in Uganda? As Prisha Tours and Travel Uganda, we offer personal service to travelers interested in exploring Uganda  in your own time. We offer cheap self drive cars for hire in Uganda, from as low as USD 40 per day. Our independent self drive travel program gives you the freedom to explore Uganda, visit the gorillas, tour national parks, villages and the top places by car. We guarantee your travel experience will be easy and stress free throughout your holiday.
